Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a bus ticket?

To book a ticket, you need to log in to your account, select the route and date, and then proceed with payment.

2. Can I cancel my bus ticket?

Yes, you can cancel your ticket within 24 hours of booking for a full refund.

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payment options such as PayPal and Google Pay.

4. Is there a discount for students?

Yes, students can avail of a 10% discount on bus tickets when they present their student ID.

5. Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, you can modify your booking by contacting customer support before the departure date.

6. How can I track my bus?

After booking, you will receive a link to track the live status of your bus on the scheduled day of travel.

7. What if I miss my bus?

If you miss your bus, please contact customer support to inquire about available options for rebooking or refunds.
